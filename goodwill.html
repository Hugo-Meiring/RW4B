The Goodwill corner



This is an opportunity to show some kindness and generosity on the day. The organisers’ goal is to supply the needy among the runners and walkers in our region – and also others in need – with running shoes, tracksuits, beanies, scarfs and T-shirts. If you have a second or third pair of these items that you seldom use and that is still in a good condition, please consider bringing it to the race and donate. A stall on the Zwartkop High School sports field, close to the Finish, will be available for these and any other sport items before and after the race.
