Friends of the Riana van Niekerk RW4B 2017


The Riana van Niekerk Run & Walk 4 Bibles is race organised and executed by the community for the community. We honour a community of friends stepping forward every year, providing the finances and volunteering their valuable time to build one of the fastest growing community races in the country. We believe that the Riana van Niekerk Run & Walk 4 Bibles will become the biggest half marathon in South Africa because of its purpose. When God is in control we can expect the unthinkable to happen in God's time. We want to thank each and every friend of this race for a valuable friendship and partnership in uniting Gauteng for Bible distribution and translation. You are the backbone of the Riana van Niekerk Run & Walk 4 Bibles.
